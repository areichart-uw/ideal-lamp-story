The scene opens with a man, bruised and severely cut up. He looks over a burning village, the place he was destined to save, yet ended up destroying.
Off in the distance he could see the remains of the house he called home. He began walkig towards it.
All came to his mind were the memories with his wife and kids, but now only ash left, as he finally stood in front of his house. Nothing more but his tears.
He knew what he had done was wrong. He knew that there was no way to fix it, and the only solution was to live up to his mistake, even if it would kill him.
